About the Campaign

Mangroves are small trees that grow along coastlines. They are powerful protectors of nature — reducing floods, storing carbon, and sheltering thousands of species. Our goal is to bring communities together to replant and protect these coastal forests.

Our Impact So Far

Thanks to our volunteers and supporters, we’ve already planted over 25,000 mangroves across several coastal villages in Indonesia. Each tree makes a difference — reducing erosion and protecting marine life.

How It Works

Every contribution and volunteer counts! Here’s how we make it happen:

  • 1. Collect mangrove seeds and nurture them in community nurseries.
  • 2. Work with local volunteers to plant the trees along coastlines.
  • 3. Monitor growth and provide care for 3 years to ensure survival.
